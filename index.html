A smarter way to heat: store off‑peak, release on‑demand

Thermal battery core

Engineered storage medium in a steel drum, optimized for high energy density and safe residential operation.

Appliance‑grade enclosure

Rectangular steel housing with analog control interface — a familiar, reliable feel for the home.

Air mixing & delivery

Three cold‑air inlets, a high‑efficiency mixer, and a heated‑air outlet deliver comfortable heat when you need it.
